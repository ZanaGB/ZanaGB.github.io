ABOUT THIS WEBSITE

This is my personal pet project, portfolio, and overall testing ground for stuff.

There is not a lot to say about the website, really. Currently it's in a very early WIP state, but you will eventually see more details here and there as it's built. I intend for this website to essentially represent all things me. My art, my photography, music doodles, opinion pieces, so on, so forth. On top of finally adding a proper ToS page for my commissions, and a way for people to check things out all in all.

I am well aware this is an unorthodox way, considering there are so many websites to do one of these things at a time, but i want a simple website, with the minimum amount of tracking, no random stuff. Just content.

On top of this being a platform for self advertisment this is also a good refresher on web technologies, and server management. This website is essentially living proof of most of my skills, as everything is configured manually by me and it exists throuth my own force.
