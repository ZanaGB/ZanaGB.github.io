Artwork Commission Terms of Service

No gore, violence, or outright pornogrpahy will be drawn under any circumstance. Although certain types of NSFW content can be drawn ( Ask for more details ).

In the event of a commission of NSFW nature, topics involving deceit, manipulation, loss of identity and/or humanity, acting over a third party without their express agreement, and others, will be rejected.

It is imperative to specify the details of the work to be comissioned through text and verify before proceeding with the payment.

The client will receive periodical status updates in the form of downscaled WIP images trhrough the creation of the artwork.

For each commission, the client is entitled to a maximum of 3 major edits before the Minimum Viable Product stage has been achieved. Once reached, only very minimal edits can be requested.

For the average image, the MVP stage is reached after the sketch cleanup before proceeding with inking. For other types of work, the MVP stage may differ. Do not hesitate to consult.

The average completion time for a picture usually hovers around 45 days. It may be more or less depending on the specifics of the commission at hand.

Payment will be split in two halves, 60% before the work has been initiated, with the later 40% happening upon work completion. Full resolution imagery and source files will be given after payment.

For more information on the actual estimate please do consult directly.
